Mas can Ferrer - Venue for the Gala Dinner

Mas Can Ferrer is located in the middle of the nature. It offers different spaces for any kind of celebrations or meetings. The Glass dining room offers wonderful views to Montseny Mountain. It is a unique place to celebrate a very special dinner.

Location: Montseny (Mountain)

Dining room capacities: Suitable for 140 pax

Price Level: average-high

Style: Catering

Vegetarian Options/Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: Yes
