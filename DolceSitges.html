Dolce Sitges is a 5 star hotel located just few kilometers away from Barcelona. Guests will expereince luxuries and views of the Mediterranean in the coastal town of Sitges. Most of the rooms features a view of the sea, in the surroundings of Garraf Natural Park.

The hotel features 4 new climatized outdoor pools working by solar energy. Hotel also has a Gym and personal trainer completely renewed with last technology, TV, Wi-Fi and music.

In addition to all the servicies available at the hotel, I would like to emphasize in the meeting rooms. Dolce Sitges features more than 2.00 square meters of meeting space, state of the art audiovisual equipment, modern facilities and meeting packages.
